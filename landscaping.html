Our approach

Soft landscaping, hardscaping, and everything between.

01

Design and planning

We help shape the layout, planting, materials and practical flow of your outdoor area so the finished space works for the way you actually live. Every site gets a thorough assessment before any work begins.

02

Build and installation

We install turf, garden beds, irrigation, paving, decking, mulch and any other landscaping features your project needs — handled in-house by our team. No subcontracting, no handoff surprises.

Finish and maintain

We complete the space to a high standard and can stay on as your ongoing garden care team. Maintenance plans are available on request — the same team, the same standard, ongoing.

What's included

Our landscaping service covers eight core areas.

Mix and match what your project needs. Most Eastern Suburbs gardens combine three or four of the below.

01

Turfing

Fresh natural grass installation for a greener, more usable lawn — chosen for your soil type, sun exposure and level of use.

Best for: tired lawns, new builds, family backyards
02

Planting and softscape

New plantings that infuse your garden with life and colour, chosen for your sun, soil and the look you're after.

Best for: bare gardens, refreshes, screening
03

Garden bed design

New beds shaped to your garden — built up, edged and planted for a lush, layered look that holds through every season.

Best for: front yards, borders, feature beds
04

Irrigation systems

Watering systems designed to keep your landscape healthy year-round, even when you're away, with smart controller options.

Best for: established gardens, new plantings, drought-prone yards
05

Decking

Timber and composite decks that connect your home to the garden, built to handle Sydney's coastal conditions and daily use.

Best for: entertaining areas, pool surrounds, level changes
06

Paving

Practical, attractive paths and outdoor surfaces from a wide range of natural stone and pavers suited to Sydney's climate.

Best for: paths, courtyards, patios, driveways
07

Mulching

Finishing mulch for healthier soil, fewer weeds and better moisture retention through Sydney's dry summer months.

Best for: garden bed refreshes, post-planting, ongoing care
08

Site clean-up and demolition

Clearing old structures, overgrowth or tired hardscape — the prep work that makes a proper makeover possible.

Best for: full garden refreshes, rentals, pre-build

Common questions

Landscaping FAQs.

Which Sydney suburbs do you service?

We work across Sydney's Eastern Suburbs, including Bondi, Bronte, Coogee, Randwick, Paddington, Woollahra, Double Bay, Bellevue Hill, Vaucluse and Rose Bay. If you're nearby and not sure, get in touch and we'll let you know.

How long until I receive a quote?

After we visit and assess your garden, you receive a detailed, itemised quote within 3 to 5 business days. The quote is no obligation.

Do you supply the plants and materials?

Yes, we supply everything from turf and mulch to plants, planters, paving and decking materials. If you'd prefer specific plants or planters, share a list and we'll factor it into the project.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. All of our landscaping team are fully licensed and insured, so your project is in capable hands from start to finish.

Can you handle the whole project, including demolition?

Absolutely. We clear out old structures, overgrown beds or tired hardscape, then take the project all the way through to a finished, planted garden.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance after the build?

Yes. Many of our landscaping clients keep us on for regular garden care once the build is complete. We'll talk through a maintenance plan if you're interested.
